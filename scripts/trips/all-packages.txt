Category: Pilgrimage & Adventure
Short Description: 9-day spiritual and scenic journey through Uttarakhand covering Kedarnath darshan by helicopter, all 5 Panch Prayag confluences, Chopta trekking, and Auli sightseeing.

=== FULL DESCRIPTION ===
An immersive 9-day Uttarakhand circuit starting from Delhi, covering the sacred Kedarnath Temple via helicopter, the breathtaking meadows of Chopta, and the ski-resort town of Auli. The tour passes through all five Panch Prayag — Devprayag, Rudraprayag, Karnaprayag, Nandprayag, and Vishnuprayag — with a night in Rishikesh before returning to Delhi. Ideal for families and spiritual seekers seeking both divine darshan and Himalayan scenery.

Day 1: Delhi Arrival → Haridwar (Approx. 5–6 Hrs Drive)
Arrival at Delhi Airport / Railway Station. Meet & greet by representative. Drive to Haridwar. Check-in at hotel. Evening leisure or optional Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri. Overnight stay at Haridwar.

Day 2: Haridwar → Guptkashi via Devprayag & Rudraprayag (Approx. 8–9 Hrs Drive)
Early morning breakfast and checkout. Drive through scenic Himalayan routes. Enroute witness Devprayag (confluence of Alaknanda & Bhagirathi) and Rudraprayag (confluence of Alaknanda & Mandakini). Arrive at Guptkashi. Dinner and overnight stay.

Day 3: Kedarnath Temple Visit by Helicopter
Early morning transfer to helipad. Helicopter ride to Kedarnath Temple. VIP Darshan, puja, meditation, and local sightseeing. Return by helicopter to Guptkashi. Dinner and overnight stay.

Day 4: Guptkashi → Chopta via Ukhimath (Approx. 4–5 Hrs Drive)
Breakfast and checkout. Drive to Chopta — the mini Switzerland of Uttarakhand. Arrive at Chopta hotel/camp. Evening at leisure with mountain views. Dinner and overnight stay.

Day 5: Chopta Sightseeing & Excursion
Breakfast at hotel. Explore Chopta's natural beauty. Optional trek to Tungnath Temple (world's highest Shiva temple) and Chandrashila summit. Views of Nanda Devi, Chaukhamba, and Kedarnath peaks. Evening free for photography. Dinner and overnight stay.

Day 6: Chopta → Auli via Vishnuprayag (Approx. 6–7 Hrs Drive)
Breakfast and checkout. Drive to Auli via Joshimath. Enroute visit Vishnuprayag (confluence of Alaknanda & Dhauliganga — 5th Prayag). Arrive Auli, check-in. Dinner and overnight stay.

Day 7: Auli Sightseeing
Breakfast at hotel. Full day sightseeing — local attractions, viewpoints. Optional: ropeway ride, cable car, nature walks. Views of Nanda Devi and Himalayan peaks. Dinner and overnight stay.

Day 8: Auli → Rishikesh via Karnaprayag & Nandprayag (Approx. 8–9 Hrs Drive)
Breakfast and checkout. Enroute Karnaprayag (Alaknanda & Pindar) and Nandprayag (Alaknanda & Nandakini). Arrive Rishikesh. Visit Ram Jhula, Laxman Jhula, Parmarth Niketan. Optional Triveni Ghat Ganga Aarti. Overnight stay.

Day 9: Rishikesh → Delhi Airport (Approx. 5–6 Hrs Drive)
Breakfast and checkout. Drive to Delhi Airport with comfort stop enroute.
